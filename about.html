About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Chelsea Slade, and I'm a Software Developer in-training. I'm currently enrolled in the Software Development program at Keyin College, with an expected graduation in April 2025.

I'm passionate about many things, including but not limited to programming, design & illustration, biological science, and literature! I have a Bachelor of Science in Biochemistry (Nutrition) from Memorial University of Newfoundland and Labrador, and frequently indulge in free online skills classes. In my free time, I enjoy walks with my husband and our energetic pup Suki along the coast of sunny Conception Bay South, and painting colourful birds and buildings that remind me of home.

I look forward to a continued career in the amazing technology industry we have here in Newfoundland and Labrador!
